I’m Dev Patel, a Fullstack Developer passionate about creating impactful digital solutions. My journey started with building static clones of platforms like Amazon, Flipkart, Disney+ Hotstar, YouTube, and Spotify, refining my frontend skills. My curiosity then led me to explore APIs, Chrome extensions, and backend development.

I’ve worked with technologies like React, Tailwind CSS, MUI, Node.js, and Express, contributing to projects such as MeTUBE, MatchupX, and Travel Diaries. Currently, I’m a Fullstack Developer intern at CarinaFashions, developing user-centric digital solutions.

I believe in collaboration, innovation, and constant learning. Solving complex problems with clean code and efficient designs is what excites me. Outside of coding, I enjoy exploring new tech, working on personal projects, or relaxing with a good book or game.
